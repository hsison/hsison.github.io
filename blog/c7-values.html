One of the most important values to me Achievement

Achievement is important to me because it gives me a sense of worth. I don't feel like I'm worthless without achievement, however when I achieve something, I feel like I'm contributing something to the world. I'm using my very little time here on earth wisely. I'm not just soaking up energy. I am doing, being, and achieving. That is what makes me feel alive.

My Values and Stereotype threat

I feel happy when I reflect on my values. I am constantly trying to better myself by learning and growing. When I don't live up to my personal expectations, that's where my upset derives from. I definitely think it could help me mediate stereotype threat if I recognized I felt it. Most of the time I don't even realize it.
The best therapy for me in most situations is exercise. If I feel sad, tired, or my brain is just running slow from doing so much homework at DBC, I exercise. It is scientifically proven that exercise benefits not only the body but also the mind. Why not benefit both?
